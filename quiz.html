Stress-Related Questions

Answer the following questions to assess your stress level:

  1. Do you often feel overwhelmed with your daily tasks?
  2. Do you have trouble sleeping due to worrying thoughts?
  3. Have you experienced changes in your appetite recently?
  4. Do you feel tense or have muscle tension frequently?
  5. Have you noticed a decrease in your ability to concentrate?
  6. Do you frequently feel irritable or easily angered?
  7. Have you experienced any unexplained physical symptoms, such as headaches or stomachaches?
  8. Do you often feel fatigued or lacking in energy?
  9. Have you withdrawn from social activities or hobbies you used to enjoy?
  10. Do you frequently experience racing thoughts or difficulty quieting your mind?
